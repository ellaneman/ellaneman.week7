Ella Neman's Website Specifications

What is the purpose of this website?

For the website I will be creating will be a online portfolio which will show case my graphic design work. I think creating a portfolio will be super useful to showcase all my work especially since I think I want to take a career path in branding.

Who is the intended audience?

The intended audience of my online portfolio will be potential employers. My portfolio can help my employers and future employers, see my work as a way to see my work ethic.
